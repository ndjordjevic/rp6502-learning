## Overview

4K autofocus digital microscope for close-up inspection of PCBs, solder joints, SMD components, and traces. Used when building or debugging boards (e.g. Alicia 1200): verifying solder quality, pin alignment, bridges/cold joints, and reading small markings.

**Two variants:**
- **EM4K-AF** — 8″ monitor, 10″ bracket/boom stand
- **EM4K-AF Flex** — 10.1″ monitor, flex arm stand (C-clamp), ring light, soldering mat

---

## Read me first

1. **Battery:** Rechargeable battery in monitor. Charge 2–3 h; continuous use ~1–2 h. For autofocus, voltage demand is higher: keep battery **>50%** or use **AC power** (recommended for long sessions).
2. **Power:** Use the included 5V adapter and plug when possible so autofocus and LEDs work reliably.
3. **Small parts:** Product contains small parts; keep away from children. Not for medical use.
4. **Lens:** If image quality drops, clean the **UV filter under the lens** with a soft cloth (e.g. glasses cloth) lightly moistened with alcohol.
5. **Screen:** Monitor is not shockproof or waterproof. Handle gently; clean with a dry, soft cloth. Do not press the screen when adjusting angle.
6. **Warranty:** 1 year manufacturer warranty; lifelong technical support. Include photos/videos of issues when contacting support (support usually replies within 12 h on business days).

## Installation (Flex)

### Flex arm stand

1. Remove screw from bottom of pole; insert cover into C-clamp, connect pole to C-clamp, tighten screw.  
2. Clamp to desk and tighten.  
3. Attach monitor arm to pole and tighten.  
4. Attach bracket to arm, secure with black ring.  
5. Slide monitor onto arm and secure pole with screw.  
6. Organise cables with cable clips on the arm.

### Ring light

- Unscrew the 3 small screws on the lens cover and attach the ring light (see manual illustration).  
- **Do not** attach the light over the focus ring/knob.

### Side/base lights (Flex)

- **Base light:** Knob on base turns base light on/off and adjusts brightness.  
- **Side lights:** Need power from monitor (USB-C on base ↔ 5V output on monitor, or from AC). See “How to make the LED lights work” in the manual.

## Quick start

1. Connect USB-C on base to 5V output on monitor (for side lights if used).  
2. Turn on the microscope.  
3. Place object on base; AF focuses in about 1–3 s.  
4. Adjust stand height for more magnification (AF runs again after distance change).  
5. **Long-press OK** to cycle Photo / Video / Split / Freeze. **Press OK** to capture photo or start/stop video.

## Focus and presets

### Autofocus (AF)

- Default mode; “AF” shown top-left.  
- Place object on base; focus completes in a few seconds.  
- **Working distance &lt; 20 mm:** AF can be slow or fail; **long-press AF** to switch to **manual focus (MF)**.

### Manual focus (MF)

- Long-press AF to enter MF.  
- Use **+ / −** on lens or remote to focus.  
- **Preset P1/P2:** At desired height, press **P1** to save focus; press **P1** again to recall. Same for **P2**. Hold P1 or P2 ~2 s to save; indicator turns green. Useful for switching between two board heights without refocusing.

### AF OSD colours (top-left)

- **White** — AF tracking or idle  
- **Yellow** — AF in progress / adjusting  
- **Green** — AF success  
- **Red** — AF failed (try MF or adjust distance)

## LED lights

- **Main LED** (around lens): Touch bar on monitor; slide to set brightness.  
- **Side lights** (on base): Need separate power — from monitor’s 5V output (USB to base USB-C) or from AC. Base knob controls side light on/off and brightness.  
- With side lights on from monitor 5V, battery drains faster; use AC for long use.

## Saving media and modes

- **Micro SD:** Insert card in slot on back of monitor. In menu: choose 13M/52M (photo), 4KP30/1080P60 (video), and freeze layout (full/left/up/four).  
- **Modes:** Long-press OK to cycle **Photo / Video / Split / Freeze**.  
- **Split:** Right = zoom in (1×→4×), Left = zoom out.  
- **Freeze:** Freeze image for comparison; choose full/left/up/four with Right, confirm with OK.  
- **Playback:** MENU → PLAYBACK → OK (photo or video mode first). Left/Right = previous/next; OK = play/pause (video).  
- **Files:** In playback, enter file management; lock/unlock/delete. Locked files are not deleted on device (can still be deleted when card is in a computer).

## Reset

- Use a ballpoint pen or similar to press the **reset pinhole** on the back for 1–2 s.

## HDMI and PC

### HDMI

- Connect HDMI cable from microscope to TV/monitor; set display to HDMI input. Image mirrors microscope screen.

### PC (UVC)

1. Connect USB cable (monitor USB-C to PC).  
2. Turn on microscope; screen shows **MSDC** (storage) or **UVC** (camera).  
3. Press **OK** to select **UVC**.  
4. On **Windows:** Open “Windows Camera” (Store if missing). Allow camera in privacy settings; disable built-in laptop camera if needed.  
5. On **Mac:** Open Photo Booth (Applications); select “VMS800S (default)”.  

**Note:** When connected to PC, only Power and OK work on the unit; photos/videos are saved on the PC, not on the Micro SD card.

### Tri-screen

- Microscope screen + HDMI display + PC (USB UVC) can be used at once for live view on three displays.

## FAQ (condensed)

1. **Won’t turn on** — Check cable and power; try reset.  
2. **AF not working** — Ensure AF mode (long-press AF to switch); if distance &lt; 20 mm, use MF.  
3. **P1/P2 not working** — Fix stand height, focus at desired position, then press P1/P2 to save; press again to recall.  
4. **Unclear image** — Check LEDs, distance, and focus; clean lens (dry soft cloth or light alcohol).  
5. **Restarts repeatedly** — Use a good **5V 2A** power adapter.  
6. **SD “card error” / “full”** — In menu: SETTING → FORMAT → OK; or format card in computer.  
7. **HDMI no image** — Microscope on; display set to HDMI input.  
8. **Red cross on screen** — Menu → MARKING → CROSS CUR → Off.  
9. **Red reference line** — Menu → MARKING → LINE SHOW → Off.  
10. **Side lights don’t work** — They need power from monitor battery or AC (see “LED lights” above).  
11. **No image on PC** — Correct USB cable; press OK for UVC; allow camera in Windows/Mac and disable built-in camera if needed.

## Use for PCB / Alicia build

- **Inspection:** Part 1 and Part 2 component check (BOM); solder joints, bridges, cold joints; pin alignment on ICs and connectors.  
- **Stability:** Tighten all stand positions (A/B/C/D on 10″ stand; clamp and arm on Flex) to avoid shake.  
- **Lighting:** Use main + ring (and base/side if needed); avoid glare; adjust brightness and WDR if needed.  
- **Focus:** For consistent height (e.g. same board zone), use P1/P2 presets. For &lt; 20 mm working distance, use MF.  
- **Documentation:** Use Photo/Video or PC UVC to record board state before/after rework.
